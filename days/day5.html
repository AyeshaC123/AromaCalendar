Day 5: Diffuser Therapy

Diffuser

A diffuser is a device that injects the air with an essential oil, often used to help individual breathe better, or spread the air of a room with a refreshing and calming scent. Filling a diffuser with lavendar oil while doing your daily activites is a great way to make your day more calming. Inhaling this scent can reduce feelings of stress and anxiety.

TO DO

1. While your doing daily activities or chores, have a diffuser with lavendar oil on in the room. You'll notice how much of an effect it can have on your overall mood and stress levels as your working.
