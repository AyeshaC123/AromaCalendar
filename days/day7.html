Day 7: Aromatic Bath Activities

Aromatic Bath Therapy

An aromatic bath is a bath where you add essential oils to the water of your bath. The combination of warm water and the aromatic aroma of a body oil infused into the bath can have wonderous effects on the body such as reducing anxiety and body strains. Aromatic baths can: Relieve pain, detoxify the body and have major respiratory benefits by inhaling the steam. Learn more about Aromatic Bath Essential Oils

TO DO:

1. Run a warm bath, and add 1 cup of Epsom Salt, 5- 10 drops of essential oil of your choice, 1 tbsp of carrier oil and 1 tbsp of castile soap or unscented shampoo. 2. Leave the bath running for ten minutes for this concoction to mix and enter your aromatic stress relieving bath.
