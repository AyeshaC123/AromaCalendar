Day 4: Lavendar Oil Therapy

Lavendar Oil Therapy

Our first type of aroma therapy is using Lavendar oil. Lavendar oil is of the commonly used/adaptable essential oils. Lavandula angustifolia oil, which is taken by extracting the distilled water from the plant, is known to induce relaxation and has been clinically researched as a treatment for stress, anxiety, depression, and maybe other illnesses.

Benefits of Lavendar Therapy

Anxiety: Based on numerous studies and research on the Lavender plant, it has been concluded that utilizing lavendar oil, in any way it interacts with the body, does significantly reduce anxiety. An example of this study is shown here: Link to research paper: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3612440/

Another notable research conducted was using lavendar oil to reduce the anxiety of being in a doctors office. A study was conducted in 2005 on 200 people that were in a dentist office awaiting for treatment. The study found that the scent of lavender lessended both their anxiety and improved mood. Link to study: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0031938405002660?via%3Dihub
