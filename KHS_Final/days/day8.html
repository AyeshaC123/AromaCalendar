Day 8: Aromatic Candle Activities

Aromatic Candle Therapy

Scented candles and room sprays are a great way to make your enviroment play a role in reducing your stress levels and anxiety. Room sprays are an easy and effected method to increase the calmness of ones space, creating a relaxing and comforting environment. It can promote sleep, reduce anxiety and and improve ones focus. It can also better ones mood and well-being. Aromatic candles play the same role, it is as easy as lighting a candle in your room while you do your daily activities. Learn more about Aromatic candles

To do:

1. Purchase an aromatic candle or room spray. Here are some examples of good ones: *Lavendar *Peppermint and Eucalyptus spray *Rosemary *Tea tree *Chamomile and Bergamot Spray 2. Spray the room spray or light the candle around the room you plan to use. Make sure not to spray too much as that can trigger nausea. 4-5 sprays in a medium sized room is fine.
